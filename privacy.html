Who We Are

FutureScaping Ltd provides drone survey, mapping, landscape design, and environmental monitoring services. This monitoring system is used to share survey outputs, interpretation notes, imagery, section profiles, and related project information with clients and project stakeholders.

Website: futurescaping.co.uk

What Personal Data We May Handle

This monitoring system is mainly a project dashboard. It is not designed to collect large amounts of personal information. We may still handle limited personal data if it is provided to us or appears in normal technical records, including:

  • name, email address, phone number, and organisation when someone contacts FutureScaping;
  • technical information such as IP address, browser type, device type, and server logs created when the service is used;
  • project notes, uploaded files, or survey metadata if they include information that can identify a person;
  • admin activity needed to maintain the monitoring system.

How We Use Information

We use information to provide the monitoring system, respond to client enquiries, maintain project records, diagnose technical issues, protect the service, and support agreed survey or reporting work.

Our Lawful Basis

We usually process information because it is necessary to provide services requested by clients, to communicate about projects, to meet legal obligations, or because FutureScaping has a legitimate interest in operating and protecting its project systems.

Who We Share Information With

We may use trusted service providers to host and operate the monitoring system, including hosting, storage, deployment, email, and website providers. We do not sell personal data.

Where project information is shared with clients, consultants, contractors, landowners, or project partners, this is done for the purpose of delivering the relevant project or monitoring work.

How Long We Keep Information

We keep project records for as long as they are needed for client work, monitoring comparisons, legal, insurance, accounting, or business record purposes. Technical logs are normally kept only as long as needed to operate and protect the service.

Your Rights
